HOW to PLAY

How good is your memory? test with this game

  • Watch out the background colour and remember it
  • Then select and tap the same colored button
  • When the next backgroundd color appears, you need to tap the previous color along with the new one
  • Keep on going as long as you can.
  • Further the level you reach better is your memory.
